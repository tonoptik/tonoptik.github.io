LEUCHTKRAFT

Audio-visual interactive installation

The installation explores "luminosity - one of the main characteristics of a quantum accelerator, indicating the intensity of collision of particles from colliding beams." It represents an artistic interpretation of hadronic collider principles used in particle physics research.

The work uses translucent screens positioned to create "a volumetric composition with a certain stereo effect" when projectors face opposite sides. The interactive component adjusts video and sound dynamics based on viewer proximity to the installation's center.

Technical Details

Equipment: 2 video projectors, 2 directional sound speakers

Software: Touchdeisigner

Materials: 8 semitransparent screens

Exhibition History

  • RGB Light Experience festival 2018, Rome, Italy
  • CYFEST 2018 festival, St. Anne's Church, St. Petersburg
