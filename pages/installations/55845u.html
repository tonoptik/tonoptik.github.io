55.845u

Audio-visual installation

55.845u represents the atomic mass of iron in atomic mass units (0.0000000000000092732796 grams). The installation explores the microscopic realm and emphasizes the vast differences between macro and micro scales, focusing on the aesthetics determined by elementary particle physics.

Technical Specifications

Equipment: 8 video projectors and speakers

Software: Touchdesigner

Exhibition History

Night of museums, Railroad Museum, St. Petersburg
